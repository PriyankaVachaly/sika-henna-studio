Aftercare Instructions

To get the best stain and longevity from your henna design:

  • Leave the paste on for at least 1-2 hours.
  • Keep the area warm and avoid water contact while the paste is on.
  • Once dry, scrape off the paste gently, do not wash it off with water.
  • Avoid soap and water on the area for 24 hours after paste removal.
  • Apply a natural oil like coconut or olive oil to preserve the stain.
